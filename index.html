About us

Established in September 2021, Women in Business is a dynamic intercampus association of IÉSEG School of Management. Comprised of 27 members across Paris and Lille our aim is to empower women in the business environment.

Our missions

Promote and contribute to gender equality in both the academic and professional environments

To prepare and support students for successful incorporation into their careers

To inform and increase students' awareness of the place of women in the professional world
